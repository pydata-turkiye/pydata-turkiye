About Our Community

PyData Turkiye is a local chapter of the global PyData community, dedicated to bringing together data enthusiasts, professionals, and researchers from Turkiye. We focus on the use and development of open-source data science tools and libraries in Python.

By fostering an inclusive and collaborative environment, PyData Turkiye aims to facilitate knowledge sharing, networking, and the advancement of data science skills through meetups, workshops, and conferences.

Whether you are a beginner or an experienced practitioner, PyData Turkiye provides a platform to learn, share, and grow in the field of data science.
